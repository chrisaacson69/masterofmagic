## Overview

A collection of tools for analyzing Master of Magic (1994) through the vault's BV and MIRR frameworks. Python gathers and computes, LLM analyzes and writes findings back to the vault.

## Vault Research Context

### BattleValue
**Source:** [BattleValue](vault: research/gaming/battle-value.md) — BV = sqrt(Attack × HP)
**Key insight:** BV/Cost is the army composition ROI metric. The unit with the highest BV/Cost ratio is the best investment given a fixed budget.
**MoM adaptation:** Multi-figure units, ranged attacks, to-hit modifiers, and special abilities all need BV extensions.

### MIRR
**Source:** [MOO1 MIRR Analysis](vault: research/gaming/moo1/mirr-analysis.md) — Modified Internal Rate of Return
**Key insight:** Compare investment quality at each decision point. Factory MIRR vs colony MIRR determines when to expand.
**MoM adaptation:** Building chains (prerequisite trees) and spell research as investment paths.

### Rule Interactions (the real challenge)
MoM's "balance through imbalance" isn't just about stats — it's about abilities that create hard counters:
- Magic Immunity shuts down all spell-based damage
- First Strike kills before retaliation
- Weapon Immunity blocks non-magical melee
- Flight avoids ground-only units entirely
- Illusionary attacks bypass armor completely

Base BV is necessary but insufficient. The analysis needs a matchup matrix: unit A vs unit B given their specific abilities and the rules that govern interaction.

## Data Source

**Wiki:** https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Master_of_Magic_Wiki
**API:** `https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/api.php`

MediaWiki infobox templates with consistent fields:
- Units: melee, ranged, defense, resist, hits, figures, abilities, build_cost/casting_cost, upkeep
- Buildings: construction_cost, upkeep, prerequisites, effects
- Spells: casting_cost, research_cost, upkeep, rarity, type, effects

## Tool Descriptions

### scraper/
Pull all entity data from the wiki into structured JSON. Parse infobox templates, handle edge cases (multi-value fields, conditional abilities, level progression tables).

### analyzer/
- **BV computation:** base BV + ability modifiers for all units
- **BV/Cost rankings:** separate for production economy (normal units) and mana economy (fantastic creatures)
- **MIRR models:** building investment chains, spell research paths
- **Matchup matrix:** unit-vs-unit effectiveness considering rule interactions (hard counters, immunities, damage type interactions)

### simulator/
Recreate the MoM combat engine. The wiki already has a battle simulator — use it as a reference/validation target. The simulator serves two purposes:
1. Validate BV predictions against actual combat outcomes
2. Generate empirical data for matchups that BV can't easily model (AoE, healing, multi-unit armies)

### data/
- `units.json` — all normal + fantastic units with full stats
- `buildings.json` — all buildings with costs, prereqs, effects
- `spells.json` — all 214 spells with costs, effects, rarity
- `matchups.json` — computed matchup results (generated by simulator)
- `bv_rankings.json` — BV/Cost rankings (generated by analyzer)

## Game Versions

- **Original (1994):** primary analysis target — the "everything is broken" baseline
- **Caster of Magic (fan rewrite):** community rebalance — compare to see what changed and why
- **Official rewrite (2022):** Slitherine's remaster — another data point for balance changes

Comparing BV/Cost rankings across versions reveals what the community and developers agreed was broken.
